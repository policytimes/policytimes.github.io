Editor's Desk

After creating history by presenting the first paperless Union Budget for FY21/22, The Centre is looking forward to accelerating the country's vaccination drive. Phase 2 of the inoculation has started from March 1, 2021, covering a larger population base as India prepares to export more vaccines to countries in need of boosting the country’s reputation in vaccine diplomacy. 

The Prime Minister in the last month has appreciated the efforts of the private sector in India and encouraged them to be self-reliant while assuring that Government interference would be at the bare minimum. The appreciation for the private sector and assurances of government regulations supporting the private sector in India has led to titans of the industry as well as industry bodies appreciating the PM’s efforts to include the private sector as a major force to push India to become a USD 5 Trillion economy in the coming few years.

Ministry of Agriculture & Farmer Welfare 

Centre approves the “Formation and Promotion of 10,000 new Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)” scheme 

Relevant to M&M- FES, FAAS

February 9, 2021

  • Aggregation of small, marginal and landless farmers into FPOs will help enhance economic strength & market linkages of farmers for enhancing their income. 

  • Government of India has launched a new Central Sector Scheme titled "Formation and Promotion of 10,000 Farmer Produce Organizations (FPOs)" with a clear strategy and committed resources to form and promote 10,000 new FPOs in the country with budgetary provision of Rs 6865 crore.
