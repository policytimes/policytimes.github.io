Editor's Desk

India celebrated its 74th Independence Day on 15 August 2020. Prime Minister Modi delivered an impassioned speech from the ramparts of the Red Fort. He shared many of our Achievements on the occasion and articulated that:

  • India has witnessed an 18% jump in FDI even during COVID-19 pandemic.
  • The Government will focus on multi-model connectivity infrastructure projects.
  • In the coming 1000 days, every village in the country will be connected with a optical fibre cable.
